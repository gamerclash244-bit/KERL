About Kerala eRaja League

The story behind Kerala's most exciting eFootball tournament.

Our Story

Born from a Passion for the Game

Kerala eRaja League (KERL) was born from a simple idea: bring Kerala's passionate eFootball community together and give them a stage worthy of their talent. From casual players to hardcore competitors, KERL is for everyone who bleeds football.

We organize structured, fair, and transparent tournaments following the World Cup format — 48 players, 8 groups, and a knockout bracket that culminates in one ultimate champion claiming the eRaja crown.

Whether you're playing on mobile or console, KERL is your arena. Represent your district, prove your skill, and write your name into Kerala's esports history.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To create a fair, inclusive, and world-class eFootball tournament experience for players across all 14 districts of Kerala. We are committed to nurturing talent, building community, and elevating the standard of competitive gaming in the state.

👁️

Our Vision

To become the most respected esports organization in Kerala, launching careers, inspiring the next generation of gamers, and putting Kerala on the national and global esports map. We envision a future where KERL is the gateway to professional esports.
