Official Rules

Tournament Rules

⚽ Match Rules

▼
  • All matches are played on official eFootball app/game.
  • Group stage: 1 leg. Knockouts: Best of 1 (QF onwards: Best of 3).
  • Match time and opponent assigned by organizers.
  • No-shows without prior notice = automatic loss.

🤝 Fair Play

▼
  • Respect all opponents and tournament officials.
  • No trash talk, abuse, or discriminatory language.
  • Violations may result in immediate disqualification.
  • Sportsmanship is valued above results.

🚫 No Cheating Policy

▼
  • Use of exploits, glitches, or hacks is strictly banned.
  • Screen recording may be requested as proof.
  • Any cheating = permanent ban from KERL tournaments.

💳 Registration & Payment

▼
  • Registration is free. Entry fee (if any) will be communicated after selection.
  • Do NOT pay anyone claiming to be from KERL without official confirmation.
  • Refunds available only in case of tournament cancellation.

📱 Device & Connection

▼
  • Players are responsible for their own device and internet connection.
  • Disconnection mid-match: replay only if both agree.
  • Device must match category selected during registration.

🏅 Selection Criteria

▼
  • Players will be selected based on experience and district representation.
  • Selected players will be contacted by phone within 7 days.
  • Waitlist players may be promoted if selected players withdraw.
