Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How do I register for KERL?

▼

Visit the Home page and scroll to the Registration section. Fill in all required details and submit. You'll be contacted by phone if selected.

Is there a registration fee?

▼

Registration is completely free. If there is an entry fee, it will only be communicated officially after you are selected, and you will never be asked to pay upfront by anyone.

Can I participate from any district of Kerala?

▼

Yes! KERL is open to players from all 14 districts of Kerala. In fact, we encourage district-wise representation for a more balanced and exciting tournament.

What devices are supported?

▼

KERL supports both Mobile (eFootball app) and Console (PlayStation or Xbox) players. Make sure you select the correct device during registration.

When will I know if I'm selected?

▼

Selected players will be contacted via phone within 7 days of the registration closing. You can also check the Selected Players section on the homepage.
